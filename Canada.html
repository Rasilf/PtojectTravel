Introduction\Welcome to my tourist trip to Canada, during this trip,I visited a group of the most famous tourist attractions,and i will present these places to you with my personal evaluation of each experince based on the beauty and development i have seen.
The best times to visit:
Spring: Moderate temperatures and flourishing nature, suitable for hiking and cycling.
Summer: A busy season with high prices, suitable for outdoor activities and beaches.
Autumn: The colors of the autumn trees are wonderful, with lower prices and less crowding.
Winter: Ideal for skiing and viewing snow-covered landscapes, with the need to prepare for very cold weather.
